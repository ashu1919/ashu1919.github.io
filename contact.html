Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with me

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while complex web applications may take 2-6 months. I provide detailed timelines after understanding project requirements.

What is your development process?

My process follows these stages: Discovery & Planning → Design & Prototyping → Development → Testing & Quality Assurance → Deployment → Maintenance & Support. I maintain regular communication throughout.

Do you provide maintenance after project completion?

Yes, I offer various maintenance plans including bug fixes, updates, security monitoring, and performance optimization. We can discuss a maintenance plan that fits your needs and budget.

What technologies do you specialize in?

I specialize in modern web technologies including React, Vue.js, Node.js, Python/Django, MongoDB, PostgreSQL, and cloud platforms like AWS and Firebase. I also work with mobile technologies like React Native and Flutter.
